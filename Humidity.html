Comparison: Humidity

Given the data on the selected countries and the time of when the data was retrieved, it shows us that countries around the equator tend to have humidity higher than 30%, but countries around -10/10 latitude tend to start to decline in humidity. Another attribute to consider is that there tends to be a higher concentration of countries with humidiy above 50% compared to countries below 50%.
