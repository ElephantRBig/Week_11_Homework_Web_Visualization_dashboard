Comparison: Wind Speed

Given the data on the selected countries and the time of when the data was retrieved, it's hard to not notice that the windspeed for the majority of the countries no matter where they are located on the latitude of the earth, experience under 8 MPH windspeed. What's interesting to look at is the gradual increase of countries having higher wind speeds as latitude increases.
