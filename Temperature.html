Comparison: Temperature

Given the data on the selected countries and the time of when the data was retrieved, it's noticeable that as countries approach 0 latitude, they tend to become warmer while some reach high degrees. As the countries move away from the equator (0 latitude), countries tend to become less warmer and more colder, but it's also interesting to point out the concentration of countries above 0 latitude compared to below 0 latitude.
